Featured Case Study

Debt Review Call Centre - 340% Increase in Qualified Enquiries

Challenge

A debt review call centre was struggling with low-quality website enquiries and poor conversion rates. Their existing website wasn't generating enough qualified leads, and many enquiries were from people who didn't meet their service criteria.

Solution

We rebuilt their website with a focus on:

  • Lead Qualification: Multi-step forms that pre-qualify leads before submission
  • Trust Building: Clear explanations of the debt review process, certifications, and client testimonials
  • Multiple Contact Points: Strategic placement of phone, WhatsApp, and form options throughout the site
  • Service-Specific Pages: Dedicated pages for different debt review scenarios
  • POPIA Compliance: All forms include proper consent mechanisms and privacy policy links
  • Mobile Optimization: Fast-loading, mobile-first design for users on the go

Key Features Implemented

Pre-Qualification Forms

Forms that ask qualifying questions upfront, reducing time wasted on unqualified leads.

Click-to-Call Integration

Prominent phone buttons on every page for instant connection with the call centre.

WhatsApp Integration

Direct WhatsApp links for mobile users who prefer messaging over calling.

Trust Signals

NCR certifications, client testimonials, and clear process explanations build credibility.

What Made the Difference

The combination of lead qualification forms, multiple contact methods, and trust-building elements created a website that not only generated more enquiries but better-quality ones. The call centre team could focus on converting qualified leads rather than filtering out unqualified ones.
